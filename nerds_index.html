About Nerds of Tachikawa

Churchy

The organizers of this meeting are all members of the church Lifehouse Tachikawa. Before we begin we share a bible verse and a few words of encouragement as well as prayer. This is all in an effort to reveal the love of God to people.

Tech Nerdy

Next, we dive into the details of a pre-determined Topic and nerd out about it. Topics can be anything, from gadgets, DIY tutorials, as long as it is technology related we are good. At the end of each meeting, we take a poll to decide what we will deal with next.

Make Merry

After our discussion is over, we have some snacks whiles getting to know each other. This is a great way to make friends with people you share common interests with. Many good friends and collaborations have been birthed from this portion of our meeting.
